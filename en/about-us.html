Concealed Studio is a young design studio established in 2022.
We provide cross-disciplinary design and undertake integrated projects.
It refers to the studio, and also refers to a mechanical institution in Chinese, signifying a rational and rigorous process of realization of ideas.
Everything has been carefully designed. All experiences, just right. To fit is the criterion, the multiple values of the work are the guiding principle.
A core team of senior designers, flexibly recruited and combined as required. We provide cross-disciplinary design and undertake integrated projects.
Inspiration is often small, but it can be made to shine. A single bright spot can light up a design, we find ideas and magnify them.
A balance of learning and experimentation based on business interests. Fewer constraints, more communication, better environment, better design.
Down-to-earth, low-profile work; no one has heard of it, but it’s astonishing. Creativity is always waiting to be discovered in the hidden places of life, and design often reveals itself.
